Mission and Vision

Mission The mission of the Panamanian Association is to provide Panamanian students at USI a space to learn, know people, and share their culture with Americans and other international students.

Vision Our vision is to maintain Panamanian students connected to other USI students, giving them a space to share their culture and have fun while learning about other’s cultures.

Founders

We're dedicated to giving you the very best of college events, with a focus on the Panamanian cultural diversity, Founded in 2019 by Hortensia Almanza, Kaelyn Moreno, and Yosep Almanza, PANAS has come a long way from its beginnings in the University of Southern Indiana. Their passion for sharing the Panamanian culture with the USI community and providing a place for Panamanian Students to get involved and explore their interest in a unique and fun way on campus drove them into the idea of creating an organization. In Fall of 2019, they turned their passion, inspiration, and hard work into the Panamanian Association, which is also called by its acronym PANAS, meaning “Friends” for most Latin-American countries, including Panama. Thanks to Hortensia, Yosep, and Kaelyn, our founders, we officially started in the Fall 2019 session, participating in the Student Involvement Fair as our first activity, and won one "Organization of the Month" award in our first year! More than learning other's culture, the Panamanian Association at USI give students the opportunity to practice Spanish, participate in activities as DIYs, soccer games, game nights, dancing, and much more.

Present
